Construction

Both Structural Engineers and Architectural Designers of Design Professional Engineering Inc. have been providing Structural Engineering Design, Residential and Commercial Design, and Project Management Services, from all types of buildings for Architects, Contractors, and Private Clients.

During our years of experiences in design and project management services we know famous sub-contractors and as per our clients request for our Project Management Services during the construction-phase of their projects, we started to construct the limited projects every year.
Those projects are managed by our professional Engineers and our subcontractors' teams that have more than 15 years of experience in the construction field.

CONFLICT OF INTEREST

Between the "Construction" phase and the supervision of Construction, Design Professional Engineering Inc. are very careful to avoid any perceived conflict of Interest.
We distinguish our role as a Structural & Architectural designers must never be compromised by the financial pressures of projects.
We always request our clients to obtain competitive quotations from the other construction companies since we like to provide to our clients just the Design part of projects.
